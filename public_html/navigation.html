Advanced Audio Processing in AVPKit

Enhance your audio with AVPKit's advanced processing features, powered by FFmpeg's libavfilter and libavcodec. AVPKit offers a range of powerful tools to apply audio effects, reduce background noise, and mix multiple audio tracks seamlessly, ensuring professional-quality audio for your applications.

Audio Effects

  • Echo: Uses the aecho filter to add depth and ambiance.
  • Reverb: Utilizes the areverb filter to simulate various acoustic environments.
  • Equalization: Employs the anequalizer filter to adjust frequency balance.
  • Background Noise Reduction: Implements the anlmdn filter to minimize unwanted background noise.
  • Dynamic Noise Suppression: Uses the dynaudnorm filter to reduce noise fluctuations.

Audio Mixing

  • Multi-Track Mixing: Combines multiple audio streams with the amix filter.
  • Audio Fades: Implements fade-in and fade-out effects using the afade filter.
  • Volume Normalization: Applies loudness normalization with the loudnorm filter.

Audio Compression

  • Efficient Compression: Compress audio files and streams without compromising quality using FFmpeg's libavcodec, supporting codecs such as AAC, MP3, Vorbis, Opus, FLAC.

(Note: These features represent a selection of available options within the SDK and are not exhaustive.)

Benefits

  • Enhanced Audio Quality: Create high-quality audio content with professional effects and noise reduction, making your applications sound polished and engaging.
  • Versatile Audio Manipulation: Easily manipulate audio tracks to suit your specific needs, whether it's for editing, production, or live streaming.

Hardware Acceleration in AVPKit

AVPKit leverages GPU acceleration to enhance processing speed and efficiency. By offloading intensive tasks to the GPU, AVPKit ensures faster performance and lower CPU usage, making it ideal for demanding applications. The SDK supports a range of technologies to ensure optimal performance across different platforms.

NVIDIA NVENC/NVDEC

Encoding & Decoding: Utilize NVIDIA’s dedicated hardware for video encoding (NVENC) and decoding (NVDEC). This offers high performance and efficiency, ideal for live streaming, video conferencing, real-time encoding, video playback, editing, and transcoding applications.

Apple VideoToolbox

Encoding & Decoding: Leverage Apple’s VideoToolbox framework for hardware-accelerated video encoding and decoding on iOS and macOS devices. This ensures efficient video processing with high performance, ideal for apps requiring video streaming, playback, and editing.

Android MediaCodec

Encoding & Decoding: Utilize Android’s MediaCodec API for hardware-accelerated video encoding and decoding, providing efficient video processing on Android devices. This is perfect for applications that require high-quality video playback, recording, and streaming.

Benefits

  • Enhanced Performance: Accelerate video and audio processing tasks with GPU support, reducing CPU load and improving efficiency.
  • High-Quality Video: Maintain high-quality video output with efficient encoding and decoding processes.
  • Scalability: Support large-scale applications and handle high data volumes with robust hardware acceleration technologies.
